\# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

\#\# Do’s  
\- Maintain a professional and respectful online presence  
\- Think before posting and verify information before sharing  
\- Use privacy settings to control who can see your content  
\- Engage positively and respectfully with others  
\- Showcase your skills, achievements, and learning experiences

\#\# Don’ts  
\- Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly  
\- Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content  
\- Do not spread misinformation or unverified news  
\- Avoid engaging in online arguments or cyberbullying  
\- Do not post content that could harm your future career or reputation
