\# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist for Students

\#\# 🔒 General Safety Tips  
\- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts  
\- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible  
\- Do not click on suspicious links from unknown sources  
\- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions  
\- Keep your devices and apps updated regularly  
\- Verify website URLs before entering personal information

\#\# 💳 Financial / UPI Safety Tips  
\- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone  
\- Do not approve unknown “collect requests” on UPI apps  
\- Always double-check the receiver’s details before sending money  
\- Avoid scanning unknown QR codes, especially from strangers

\#\# 📱 Responsible Usage Tips  
\- Limit the personal information you share on social media  
\- Be cautious while downloading apps from unknown sources  
\- Log out from shared or public devices after use

\#\# 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime  
\- Report cybercrime at: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
\- Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (available 24x7)
